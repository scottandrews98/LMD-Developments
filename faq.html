FAQ About Contact Properties Home ☰
What do I do if something in the house requires maintenance?
Answer: Contact Liam on email or mobile
Do I pay my rent weekly, monthly or every few months?
Answer: Depending on the cost of the property, you will pay in three monthly instalments. For example; if the property has a monthly cost of £450, you will make a payment for the first three months of £1,350.
Will the cost of my rent change?
Answer: No. you will sign a contract at the beginning of your tenancy stating how much you will need to pay.
Who do I need to contact in case of an emergency? For example; lost key.
Answer: Contact Liam on email or mobile
What do I do if I cannot pay my rent on time?
Answer: Contact Liam on email or mobile and explain the reason why you cannot pay your rent on time.
What happens if I damage the property in any way?
Answer: You will need to contact Liam, explain what has happened and pay for the repair to the property. This could result in you losing your deposit on the house. This is subject to the terms and conditions of your contract and the cost of the damage to the property.
Will you hold house viewings for potential new tennants?
Answer: Yes, you will be notified 24 hours in advance of a potential house viewing. You will be expected to make sure the house is clean and tidy.
What if I want to stay in the same property for the following year?
Answer: Contact Liam on email or mobile as soon as you come to this decision. Make sure that you and the people sharing the proprty have all agreed to this or that you have found suitable replacements in time.
Who are the properties available for?
Answer: Families, professionals and students.
When am I likely to get a move in date?
Answer: This dependant when all the relevant housing documentation has been completed by each house member that is moving in. It is recommended that the housing documentation and referencing is completed as soon as possible.
